FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you sell veneer to the Public?

No, we are not a retail veneer provider.

How can I sell my logs to Thiesing Veneer?

Theising Veneer does not buy logs.

What are some common Thicknesses cut at Thiesing Veneer?

1/51" to 1/16" or .5mm to 1.5mm

Why do you irrigate your logs?

To keep the logs from drying out and cracking open during the warm seasons.

Where do Thiesing Veneer products go?

With the obvious exception of Antarctica, the veneer we produce is used on every continent.

What is the veneer used for?

Veneer is used for panels, doors, furniture, cabinets, and even guitars.

Where do the logs come from?

We mostly produce logs from the Midwest, east, and southern portions of the USA. We occasionally process logs from Hawaii, and the Pacific west coast.

Are you FSC Certified?

No, but we can sign a "chain of custody outsourcing agreement" to keep a certified company compliant with FSC.
